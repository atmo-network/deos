# DEOS Wiki

## Summary

DEOS is a forkable runtime framework for programmable economies: token issuance, protocol-owned liquidity, routing, staking, governance, and automated actors become one deterministic institutional machine. TMCTOL is the first standard on top of it: a mint-only curve plus treasury-owned liquidity, fee burn, bucketed policy, and bounded governance control.

The meme: DEOS replaces discretionary DAO treasury management with deterministic economic circuits.

This wiki is a self-contained knowledge product for understanding that framework. It is derived from the project truth layer, but its reading path should stay inside the wiki: pages explain concepts directly and link to other wiki pages instead of requiring a detour into source documents.
