# Who DEOS Is For

## Summary

DEOS is not primarily a retail app. It is a framework for teams that want to launch protocol economies with explicit liquidity, automation, governance, staking, and client truth boundaries.

## Primary audiences

- **Ecosystem founders:** teams launching a sovereign token economy and needing a forkable runtime baseline.
- **Substrate / Polkadot builders:** teams that want pallets, runtime patterns, scripts, and a reference client instead of starting from a blank template.
- **Tokenomics designers:** teams that need executable economic physics rather than a spreadsheet-only model.
- **Protocol operators:** teams that need deterministic automation, validation gates, and bounded upgrade surfaces.
- **Client/indexer builders:** teams that need a clear split between on-chain truth and materialized views.

## What they get

DEOS gives those teams a foundation layer: runtime services, TMCTOL as a first economic standard, wiki/docs contracts, operator scripts, and a reference client.

It does not give them a finished consumer product, guaranteed market demand, permissionless collators at launch, or a universal DAO platform.
